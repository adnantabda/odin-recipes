Cabbage Dish

To make this flavorful dish, you'll simply cook onions, carrots, cabbage, and potatoes in olive oil seasoned with cumin and turmeric. If you like, add a little ginger and garlic. "My Ethiopian friend brought this dish to a potluck and I've been making it ever since," says stamarex. "It is healthy and delicious. Do not add liquid. The cabbage and potatoes release enough moisture on their own."

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Turmeric
  • Curry powder
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps To prepare This Dish

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, minced ginger, and minced garlic to the pan.
  3. Sauté until the onions become translucent and the mixture becomes fragrant.
  4. Add shredded cabbage and sliced carrots to the pan.
  5. Sauté for a few minutes until the cabbage starts to wilt.
  6. Sprinkle turmeric, curry powder, salt, and black pepper over the cabbage mixture.
  7. Stir well to evenly coat the cabbage and vegetables with the spices.
  8. Cover the pan and cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cabbage and carrots are tender.
  9. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  10. Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish or with injera or rice.
